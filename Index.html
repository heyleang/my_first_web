"Good morning" From Mang ChhunLeang

Hello my name is sana. My grandfather gave me this name. I love my name. I am eighteen year old. And studying at Institude of technology of cambodia. I live in perk village, thmor pich commune, tboung khmum district, tboung khmum province. In my family, we are three members. My father is a farmer and my mother work in banking. I love my family with the core of my heart. I get up early in the morning at 7:00am and sleep at 12:00am. I am very punctual and humble. I do take proper care of my time and try not to waste my time. My favorite subject are math and physic. I like to pritice maths everyday. My hibbies are playing game, listen to music and watching movies. I always focus on my studies and my b career. I try to help people when I can. I am very humble and I believe in being polite with people. I believe that if everyone spread love and kindness, then this would make the world a better place.
